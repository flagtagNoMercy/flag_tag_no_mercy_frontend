Flag Tag

  • The rules are simple.
  • Pick an area to play in.
  • Each player lays a virutal flag.
  • Try to be the first player to reach another player's flag.
  • Points are awarded in the order of arrivals. The first player to get to a flag gets the most points, the last player gets the least.
  • You can move your flags location, but first you have to go back to where you left it.
  • The player with the most points after the time limit is up wins.
